FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about Starcat for GitHub on the Mac App Store.

Which systems does Starcat support?

Starcat supports macOS 15 and later. It is available on the Mac App Store for Apple Silicon and Intel Macs.

How do I get the Pro subscription?

Pro is available through Apple In-App Purchase. Open Starcat, go to Settings → Pro, and subscribe. Your subscription is managed through your Apple ID.

How do I manage or cancel my Pro subscription?

All subscriptions are managed by Apple. Open the App Store app, click your account, and go to Account Settings → Subscriptions to view, change, or cancel your Starcat Pro subscription.

How do I restore my Pro purchase on a new Mac?

Open Starcat, go to Settings → Pro, and tap "Restore Purchases." Your Apple ID will verify your subscription and unlock Pro features.

Where is my data stored?

Starcat is local-first. Repo cache, tags, notes, and status are stored in local SQLite on your Mac. GitHub tokens and AI provider keys are stored securely in your Mac's Keychain.

Does Starcat host my AI keys?

No. Starcat follows a BYOK (Bring Your Own Key) model. You configure your own AI provider keys, which are stored locally in your Mac's Keychain and never sent to Starcat servers.

Will Starcat support iPhone or iPad?

The short-term focus is making the macOS app stable and useful. Future platform plans will be announced when available.
